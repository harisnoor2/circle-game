watch the dot

instructions

when you click start, a blue dot starts making rounds around the dotted circle

occasionally (~15s by default) the circle does a double jump. that is, it skips one of the red circles

when that happens, you must click the "double jump" button within a second to gain a point

clicking outside of this one second interval or multiple times within the interval loses you point(s)

there are settings on the right that control the number of dots, speed, size, and (approximate) skip interval

the skip interval setting does not strictly set the double jump to time intervals, it only sets the probability of a skip to occur around that time interval
